About Dhas Education

Founded in 2022 and officially registered in 2024 under the Ministry of Economic Development and Trade of the Maldives (Registry No. C13562024), Dhas Education is dedicated to providing high-quality online education for everyone across the Maldives.


Our mission is simple: to deliver clear, easy-to-understand education that empowers individuals from all walks of life. Whether you're looking to develop new skills or enhance your knowledge in a particular field, we have a course for you. Our diverse offerings include:


  • Graphic Design
  • Business & Office Skills
  • Personal Development
  • Creative & Language Skills
  • Web Development and much more.

Since our inception, we’ve reached students from all corners of the country. Our students consistently commend us for our engaging teaching methods that make complex concepts easy to grasp. At Dhas Education, we pride ourselves on providing personalized support, tailoring our approach to each student's unique needs to help them progress confidently.


Join us at Dhas Education, and take the first step towards achieving your personal and professional goals.
